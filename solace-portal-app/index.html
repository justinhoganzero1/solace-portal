About Solace Portal

Built by developers, for everyone. We believe powerful software creation should never require a CS degree.

🎯

OUR MISSION

Solace Portal was founded on a single idea: every person with a great app concept deserves the tools to ship it. We eliminate the gap between vision and product by giving non-technical founders the same power as a full dev team — driven entirely by AI.

📚

THE STORY

Founded in 2024 by Justin Hogan, Solace Portal grew out of frustration with slow, expensive, and fragmented app development pipelines. What started as a personal Flutter scaffolding tool evolved into a full-stack AI studio used by founders, indie developers, and enterprise teams across 40+ countries.

⚙️

THE TECH

Solace Portal runs on a proprietary multi-model AI engine that orchestrates GPT-class language models, code synthesis agents, and real-time Flutter compilation. Our AI agents — Belinda and Sven — are trained on over 2 million open-source Flutter, Android, and iOS repositories.
