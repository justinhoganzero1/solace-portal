1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At Solace Portal, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. Designed by a team of specialists over 3 years of intensive engineering, our platform is built with privacy at its core. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered app builder.

Your code is your intellectual property. We will never use your projects, prompts, or generated code to train our AI models without your explicit consent.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Account credentials (securely hashed)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe - we never store full card details)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Feature usage and interaction patterns
  • App generation metrics (anonymized)

2.3 What We DON'T Collect

  • Your actual code or app content
  • Specific prompts you enter for AI generation
  • Personal data from minors under 13

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely for:

  • Service Provision: Creating and maintaining your account, processing payments
  • Security: Detecting fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Improvement: Analyzing aggregated, anonymized usage to enhance our platform
  • Communication: Sending important updates, security alerts, and optional newsletters

We do NOT: Sell your data to third parties, use your code for AI training, or share personal information with advertisers.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard measures:

  • Encryption: All data in transit uses TLS 1.3. Sensitive data at rest is encrypted with AES-256
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access controls and employee background checks
  • Regular Testing: Penetration testing and vulnerability assessments

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you have rights to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Objection: Opt out of certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at justinbretthogan@gmail.com

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Essential session management (login state)
  • Anonymous analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Fraud prevention

You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate with trusted third parties:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Google: Analytics and OAuth login
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple
  • Hosting Provider: Secure cloud infrastructure

Each partner is vetted for privacy compliance and only receives data necessary for their specific function.

8. International Data Transfers

As an Australian-based service, your data may be processed in Australia and other jurisdictions with adequate data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers.

9. Children's Privacy

Solace Portal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately. Parents/guardians should supervise minors aged 13-18.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account or 2 years after last activity
  • Payment records: As required by tax laws (typically 7 years)
  • Logs: 90 days for security purposes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be notified via email.
